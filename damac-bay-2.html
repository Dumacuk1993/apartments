A Couture-Inspired Paradise in Dubai Harbour with Exclusive Skycrest Collection Residences and World-Class Amenities.
DAMAC Bay 2 by Cavalli is a luxurious seafront project in Dubai Harbour, offering exclusive Skycrest Collection residences with stunning sea views. The project features world-class amenities such as a Cavalli Museum, tropical treatment rooms, art & fashion studio, and infinity swimming pools. The elegant lobby with Roberto Cavalli interior design sets the tone for the upscale living experience. The location is prestigious, close to major landmarks and airports. The project includes luxury apartments, branded duplexes, and private pools on terraces. Residents can enjoy a couture-inspired paradise with lush greenery and Cavalli DNA. The project is set to be completed by January 2028, offering a unique lifestyle in a vibrant waterfront district.
