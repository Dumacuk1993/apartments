Experience the Ultimate Luxury Living in Dubai Harbour.
DAMAC Bay by Cavalli is a luxurious seafront residential concept in Dubai Harbour, consisting of three stunning towers rising 42 storeys high. The development blurs the line between true luxury and maritime bliss, offering exquisite Cavalli-inspired interiors and breathtaking sea views from every unit. Located in the vibrant Dubai Harbour district, DAMAC Bay provides residents with a premier, multifaceted luxury maritime experience. Amenities include a private beach, three infinity pools, a rooftop opera pavilion, and a Cavalli lounge. The development also features nautical-themed designs, a virtual reality school, and a water fountain synchronized with opera performances. For those seeking the ultimate luxury, super-luxury units on the higher floors offer commanding views of the sea and shore. Experience the essence of an untamed ocean and set sail into majesty at DAMAC Bay by Cavalli.
