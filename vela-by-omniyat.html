Unparalleled Indoor-Outdoor Living Spaces and Five-Star Service
VELA, a project by OMNIYAT, is a luxurious waterfront development in Dubai offering 38 stunning residences with unparalleled indoor-outdoor living spaces. The tower rises to 150 meters and includes sumptuous penthouses, Sky Residences, and Marina Residences. Each residence features floor-to-ceiling windows, vast terraces, and L-shaped corner pools with views of the marina, Burj Khalifa, and Downtown Dubai. The Sky Palace, spread over three floors, offers two swimming pools, grand terraces, a private gym, and a majlis. Residents enjoy exclusive access to amenities such as an infinity lap pool, triple-height gym, cinema room, children's play areas, meeting rooms, and a private spa suite. Managed by Dorchester Collection, VELA provides exceptional five-star service and a lifestyle of comfort and ease. The project embodies architectural excellence, with design consultants Foster + Partners and interior designers Gilles & Boissier creating a harmonious and sophisticated living experience. VELA is a celebration of contemporary waterfront living, offering a unique blend of beauty, sophistication, and luxury in the heart of Dubai.
